Tournament Overview

The Mumbai Premier Bash is the city's most prestigious corporate and amateur cricket league, bringing together 32 elite teams to compete at the hallowed grounds of Wankhede. This 10-day extravaganza celebrates the spirit of Mumbai's cricket culture with professional-grade officiating, high-definition streaming, and an atmosphere of pure competitive excellence.

Tournament Guidelines

General Player Eligibility expand_more
  • Players must be aged 18 or above at the time of registration.
  • No professional IPL/International players are allowed in the Amateur category.
  • Valid ID proof (Aadhar/Passport) is mandatory for verification.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a player represent multiple teams? add
No, per MCA guidelines, a player registered with one team cannot play for another team within the same edition of the Mumbai Premier Bash.
What is the refund policy for entry fees? add
Cancellations made 15 days prior to the tournament start are eligible for a 70% refund. No refunds are provided thereafter.
Is the tournament broadcasted online? add
Yes, all matches starting from the Qualifiers will be streamed live on the SmartSportz.in platform and our official YouTube channel in full HD.
