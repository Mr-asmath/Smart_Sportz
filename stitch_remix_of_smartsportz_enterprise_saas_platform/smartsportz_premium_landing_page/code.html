Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using the SmartSportz platform.

How do I register my team for a tournament? expand_more
To register, simply click on the 'Explore Tournaments' button, select your desired event, and click 'Register Now'. You'll be prompted to create an account and fill in your team details and player roster.
Can I manage multiple sports on one account? expand_more
Yes! SmartSportz supports multi-sport organizations. From your administrative dashboard, you can create and manage tournaments for over 15 different sport categories simultaneously.
Is there a mobile app for live scoring? expand_more
Absolutely. We provide a dedicated scorer's app for tournament officials to input real-time match data, which syncs instantly with the web dashboard for fan viewing.
